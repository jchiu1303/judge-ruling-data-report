Methodology & caveats

Data CourtListener RECAP search: immigration bond in 12 federal districts (CA, NY, TX), filed 2016+.

Parties Plaintiff = detainee / petitioner; defendant = U.S. government (habeas framing).

Decisions Parsed from RECAP snippets via regex — not full docket review. "Not parsed" = no grant/deny language in snippet; see status detail.

Boyd scores Federal judicial ideology (Boyd et al.); matched by judge name + district.

  • Immigration bond ≠ state criminal bail — do not compare directly to NY/TX pretrial tables below.
  • Repeat petitioner matching by name can false-positive on common surnames.
  • Partial dataset until 10-year refetch completes — note date range on any published analysis.
  • No causal claims between release rates and crime statistics.
